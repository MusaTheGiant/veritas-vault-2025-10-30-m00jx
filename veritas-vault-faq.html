The Basics

New to Veritas Vault? Start here to understand what we do and how it works.

What is Veritas Vault?

Veritas Vault is a DeFi (Decentralized Finance) protocol where you stake VVA tokens and earn up to 1.32% daily through automatic rewards paid 3 times per day (every 8 hours). Your money stays in your wallet at all times, not in someone else's account.

What does "Veritas" mean?

Veritas is the Latin word for "truth." It represents our commitment to 100% transparency and honesty in everything we do.

Who founded Veritas Vault?

Nick Alan is the founder of Veritas Vault. He has 26 years of experience in the industry and is a public, known figure (not an anonymous developer). Barry Monies, a Homeland Security advisor, serves as the project's cybersecurity expert and advisor.

When was Veritas Vault launched?

Veritas Vault was launched in August 2025. This is how early you are! Do not delay, get started today, build like crazy and see where you will be in the next 3, 6, and 12 months!

How does VVA token staking work?

You buy VVA tokens with DAI stablecoin, then stake them in the smart contract. Once staked, your tokens automatically earn rewards up to 0.44% every 8 hours (1.32% daily). The rewards compound automatically, meaning your earnings grow faster over time.

What does "get paid 3 times daily" mean?

Simple: the smart contract pays you rewards every 8 hours. That's 3 payouts every 24 hours. You can claim your rewards whenever you want. No waiting, no lockups.

What are VVA and sVVA tokens?

VVA is the main token you buy and hold. sVVA (staked VVA) is what you get when you stake your VVA tokens. sVVA is what earns you the 3x daily rewards. You can unstake and convert sVVA back to VVA anytime.
