Get Started with Veritas Vault

Follow this simple step-by-step guide to set up your account and start earning passive income 3 times daily through DeFi staking

Your Roadmap to Success

Follow these 5 simple steps to begin your journey

1

Set Up Your Web3 Wallet

Download and install a Web3 wallet that is compatible with the BNB Chain. We recommend MetaMask, but you can also use TrustWallet, Rabby Wallet, TokenPocket, or any other BNB Chain-compatible wallet.

New to crypto wallets? Visit metamask.io to download the browser extension or mobile app. It's free, secure, and takes just 2 minutes to set up.

2

Add VVA Tokens to Your Wallet

Switch your wallet to the BNB Chain network, then import the VVA, sVVA, and DAI tokens using the contract addresses below. Simply click the copy button next to each address and paste it into your wallet.

3

Fund Your Wallet

Add at least $107 worth of BNB to your wallet. This covers:

  • $100 - Will be swapped for DAI to purchase VVA tokens
  • $7 - Covers BNB Chain transaction fees (gas fees)

You can purchase BNB directly through your wallet using a debit/credit card, or transfer it from a crypto exchange like Valr, Binance, Coinbase, or Kraken.

4

Connect & Create Your Account

Copy the Veritas Vault referral link that you received from your Sponsor, and paste it into the browser address bar where your Web3 wallet is installed.

  • Open your MetaMask wallet app
  • Tap the "Browser" tab at the bottom
  • Paste your Sponsor's Veritas Vault referral link in the address bar at the top
  • Click/Tap the "Connect" button when it appears
  • You'll see a WELCOME pop-up with your Sponsor's Wallet ID
  • Click/Tap the green "Create Your Veritas Vault Account" button
  • Confirm and pay the small gas fee to complete registration
5

Claim Your Staked Tokens After 24 Hours

After you stake your VVA tokens, they will go into a 24-hour holding balance. This is a CRITICAL STEP that many new members miss!

Important: You must return to your Veritas Vault dashboard after 24 hours and click/tap the "Claim" button to convert your VVA tokens into sVVA (Staked VVA). Only after claiming will you begin receiving staking rewards.

How the Claiming Process Works
  • After buying and staking VVA tokens, wait 24 hours
  • Return to your Veritas Vault dashboard
  • Click/Tap the "Claim" button
  • Your VVA tokens will be converted to sVVA (Staked VVA)
  • You'll automatically start earning rewards every 8 hours (3 times daily)
  • Rewards compound automatically - no further action needed!

Once you claim, your staking is complete and rewards begin automatically!
